About Us:

Charlotte Latin is a college preperatory private school in Charlotte, North Carolina. Tied with another school in Raleigh, it is the best school in North Carolina, and many of its students have graduated to ivy league schools. We believe in raising our students to the top through civil education, and we teach them a wide variety of skills.
